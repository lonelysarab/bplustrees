How do I use Samba?

Running the Animation

  • Press the start button to begin the animation.
  • Press suspend to susppend the animation at any time.
  • Press resume to continue after pressing suspend, after a pause in the animation, or after answering a question.
  • Press step to view the animation frame by frame.
  • Press quit to exit the Samba viewer.

Animation Speed

Use the scrollbar in the bottom left region of the control panel to control animation speed. Moving the cursor towards the left slows the animation. Moving the cursor to the right speeds up the animation.

Rewinding the Animation

After the animation has run to the end, the user may adjust the rewind scrollbar to restart the animation from any point. Adjust the scrollbar to the desired location, then press the rewind button. Warning: Very long animations may take require take a minute or two to rewind.

Quiz Mode

Some algorithm animations have accompanying questions. Click the quiz mode checkbox to turn the questions on or off and press start or resume. Note: All questions pause the animation. Press resume after each question to continue viewing.

Controls in the Animation Window

Each animation window has control buttons bellow the canvas.

  • Press up, down, right, or left to pan in the window.
  • Press in to magnify the animation.
  • Press out to zoom out.
