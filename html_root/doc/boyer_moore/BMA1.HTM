Once we start the right-to-left pass through the pattern, we continue until a mismatch occurs between a character in the master string and a character in the pattern. When that occurs, the rightmost character of the pattern is realigned to a position that is obtained by adding the index (in the master string) at which the mismatch occurred to the maximum of the entry in the two realignment arrays.

Note: If your master string is relatively long, it will not fit conveniently into the graphic window. Shrinking it will make the font too small to read, so you'll have to "pan" the data structure instead. Merely hold the mouse down in the graphic window, drag it, and the data structure will move along with it -- allowing you to see portions that were originally outside the window.
