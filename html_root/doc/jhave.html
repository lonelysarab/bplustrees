Above you will find a pull-down menu of viewable algorithms for your class. Each visualization is either a smooth animation, in the Samba or in the Animal visualizer, or a slideshow of discrete snapshots, in the Gaigs visualizer. Simply select an algorithm from the pull-down menu and click the view algorithm button. Then follow the directions given for the respective visualizer. Some algorithms can share the data. To take advantange of this option, click the use existing data box after viewing the algorithm.
