In the quick sort algorithm being visualized here, the pivot is always taken from the low index of the subarray being partitioned. Snapshots of the array are displayed after each call to the partition algorithm. The picture in the upper left corner shows the actual data in the array being manipulated. The picture in the upper right corner displays the relative magnitudes of the data in bar graph form. The lower left corner of the window shows the status of the stack frames on the system stack as this particular recursive call is made. Finally, the picture in the lower right corner shows a run-time trace diagram of the recursive calls that have been made to this point in the algorithm's execution.
